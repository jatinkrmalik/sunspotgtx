Our Story

Founded in 1986 by Mr. Deepak Kumar Malik, Deepak Electrical Industries (India) began with a simple vision — to manufacture electrical appliances that Indian families could trust. From a small workshop in Delhi, we have grown into a respected name in the electrical manufacturing industry.

Operating under the brand name Sunspot, we specialize in producing ISI-certified electric room heaters, exhaust fans, table fans, cooler fans, immersion water heaters, pumps, and industrial heating elements. Every product that carries the Sunspot name is built with the same commitment to quality that Mr. Malik established four decades ago.

Today, our manufacturing unit is located in the DSIIDC Industrial Area, Bawana, North West Delhi — a hub of industrial excellence. With a dedicated team and modern infrastructure, we continue to serve wholesalers, retailers, and businesses across India with reliable, affordable electrical solutions.
