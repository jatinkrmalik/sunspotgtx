## Summary

Deepak Electrical Industries (India) is a **small-scale manufacturer** (est. 1986) based in the Bawana Industrial Area of North West Delhi. Run by proprietor **Deepak Kumar Malik**, the company operates under the **"Sunspot"** brand and specializes in **ISI-marked household and industrial electrical appliances** — primarily room heaters, exhaust fans, table fans, cooler fans, pumps, and related heating elements. The company has a modest operation with up to 10 employees and annual turnover in the range of ₹40 Lakh to ₹1.5 Crore. They maintain active B2B listings on IndiaMart, TradeIndia (since 2009), and ExportersIndia, and also retail some electronics accessories. The business has been operating for nearly 40 years, indicating stability in its market niche.
