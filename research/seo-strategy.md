## Executive Summary

Deepak Electrical Industries has a significant first-mover advantage in digital presence. Most small electrical appliance manufacturers in Delhi NCR have minimal or no SEO-optimized websites. The B2B manufacturing SEO landscape in India rewards **specificity, technical detail, and trust signals** over generic marketing content.

**Key principles for this strategy:**
- Target **buyer-intent keywords** (procurement managers searching for suppliers), not high-volume generic terms
- Build **one dedicated page per product category** with full technical specifications
- Establish **trust signals** visible on every page (year founded, factory location, certifications, real photos)
- Optimize for **local search** (Delhi NCR) and **national B2B search** (India-wide)
- Focus on **inquiries and RFQs**, not raw traffic volume

**Expected timeline:** Long-tail product keywords can rank in 8-12 weeks. Competitive category terms take 4-8 months. Significant lead flow typically begins at 3-6 months.

```
├── /industries-served/
│   ├── /industries-served/residential/
│   ├── /industries-served/commercial/
│   └── /industries-served/industrial/
├── /blog/
├── /contact/
├── /request-quote/
└── /sitemap.xml
```

### Key Architecture Principles

1. **One page per product category** — Each product line gets its own dedicated URL with unique content, specifications, and keyword focus
2. **Flat navigation** — Product pages should be 1-2 clicks from homepage, not buried deep
3. **Product categories in top navigation** — Buyers search by product, not by "About Us"
4. **Every page reachable in 3 clicks** — Google's crawler should reach any page from any other page within 3 clicks
5. **Separate URL per product** — Not one "Products" page listing everything

### Homepage Structure

- **H1:** "Deepak Electrical Industries — Electrical Appliance Manufacturer in Delhi Since 1986"
- Opening paragraph: What you make, where you make it, who you serve
- Product category grid with images and links (all 8 categories)
- Trust signals bar: "Est. 1986 | New Delhi | ISO Certified | Made in India"
- Brief "Why Choose Us" section (38+ years experience, manufacturing capability, quality)
- Client logos or industries served
- CTA: "Request a Quote" button above the fold
- Contact information with phone, email, WhatsApp
- Google Maps embed of factory location

### Product Page Structure (Template for Each Category)

Each product page should include:

1. **H1 with primary keyword:** e.g., "Heat Convector Manufacturer in Delhi — Electric Room Heaters"
2. **Opening paragraph:** What you manufacture, where, capacity, markets served
3. **Product specifications table:**
   - Power rating (watts)
   - Voltage
   - Size/dimensions
   - Material
   - Weight
   - Color options
   - Speed/RPM (for fans)
   - Frequency (50 Hz)
   - Certification marks
4. **Available models/variants** with individual specs
5. **Product images** (multiple angles, real factory photos)
6. **Applications/Use cases** — where and how the product is used
7. **Quality & certifications** section
8. **MOQ, lead time, payment terms** (trade information)
9. **Downloadable spec sheet** (PDF — but also have HTML specs on page)
10. **FAQ section** (3-5 common questions)
11. **CTA: "Request a Quote"** form or button
12. **Related products** links

### About Page Structure

- Company history (founded 1986, Mr. Deepak Malik, growth story)
- Factory/infrastructure details (size, machinery, capacity)
- Monthly production capacity numbers
- Employee count
- Quality control process
- Certifications with images
- Real factory photos (exterior, production floor, machinery, team)

- Include relevant product images with alt text
- Link internally to relevant product pages
- Include a CTA at the end ("Need bulk supply? Contact us for a quote")
- Answer specific questions buyers actually search for
- Be written for the target audience (wholesalers, distributors, procurement managers)

### Content That Builds Trust

- **Certification pages** — Dedicated page for each certification (ISO, BIS, etc.) with certificate image, validity, what it covers
- **Infrastructure page** — Factory size, machinery list, production capacity, quality control process
- **Case studies** — Document how specific clients/distributors achieved results with your products
- **Video content** — Factory tour, product demonstrations, manufacturing process (55% of B2B buyers say video is most helpful)

### Content Rules

- **Write about what you know** — manufacturing processes, product quality, industry experience
- **Be specific** — include numbers, specifications, capacity figures
- **Don't write generic marketing content** — no "we are the best" without proof
- **Avoid keyword stuffing** — write naturally, include keywords where they fit
- **Update regularly** — add new blog posts monthly, update product pages as needed
